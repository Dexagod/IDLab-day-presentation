why does your device not work for you?

our data should work for us
in the decentralized Web ecosystem

this requires governance
of our data on the Web

  • we require control of our data
    • legislative power
  • we require availability of data
    • executive power
  • we require detection of misuse
    • judicial power

flexibility of data governance
enables flexbility in integration

  • if our goal is enabling integration
    of our data with the Web ecosystem
  • our data governance must reflect
    this requirement for flexibility

entities define rules and preferences

this represent legislative power

data spaces expose flexible interfaces

this represent executive power

agents detect misuse through receipts

this represent judicial power

basing governance of data on
requirements of a decentralized Web

  • rules and preferences provide control
  • flexible interfaces ensure availability
  • data receipts enable detecting misuse

decentalizing integration of our data
through flexibility of governance

  • governance centered around the user
